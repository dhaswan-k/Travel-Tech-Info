Traffic Signs and Signals

Traffic Signs

Regulatory signs are used to inform drivers of traffic laws or regulations that must be obeyed.

Warning signs are used to alert drivers to conditions ahead that may require a reduction of speed or an action in the interest of safety and efficient traffic operations.

Informational signs provide information about directions, distances, services, or other points of interest.

Traffic Signals

The traffic signal consists of three lights:

Red: Indicates stop.

Yellow: Warns that the signal is about to turn red. Prepare to stop.

Green: Allows vehicles to proceed.
